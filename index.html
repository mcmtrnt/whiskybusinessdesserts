OUR STORY

Creating Sweet Memories

Whisky Business & Desserts is a home business that makes and delivers gourmet desserts and chocolates. We believe every celebration deserves something special. Our passion for baking and attention to detail ensures that each creation is not just a dessert, but a memorable centerpiece for your special occasion.

We specialize in:

  • Custom designed cakes
  • Elegant cupcakes and cookies
  • Delicious and beautiful chocolates

Perfect for all occassions:

  • Weddings
  • Birthdays
  • Baby & Bridal Showers
